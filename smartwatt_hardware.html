⚡ SmartWatt — Hardware Prototype

Room node (current sensing) + per-floor voltage reference. Pedagogical wiring, not a PCB netlist.
⚠ Non-invasive design: the SCT-013 clamp reads current from the magnetic field around the wire — installation needs no mains disconnection. Relay control is optional, admin-only, and not in the pilot.

A · Room Node — current-sensing front-end

B · Per-floor Voltage Ref

C · Physical layout — mounted near the room distribution box

The clamp reads the magnetic field around the conductor; the burden resistor turns CT current into a small AC voltage, and the bias divider centres it on 3V3/2 so the ESP32 ADC can sample it. Energy (kWh) is accumulated on-device.
